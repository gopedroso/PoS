PoS GTM · Sales investment — Executive summary

  1. Total Addressable Market

    The modeled market is enormous: on the order of ~94k stores, ~US$2.54B/yr offline GMV, and ~US$120M/yr revenue potential—large enough to warrant its own GTM thesis (BR + AR + MX in this export).

  2. Segments & motions

    That opportunity spreads across VSB/SMB, Upper SMB (Escala-like), and UpMarket (Next-like)—so one channel will not carry the whole file; we need a deliberate PLG + SLG mix tuned by segment.

  3. Strategic whitespace

    High PoS GMV is the real whitespace: only ~10% of the pool naturally overlaps Core coverage today (Escala + Next/Evolución)—the rest requires dedicated PoS capacity, not borrowed account lists.

  4. Economics

    The model justifies a sales-led bet when we sell the full stack (PoS + offline): total payback sits near 2-3 months at modeled adoption—versus PoS-only at many months—so bundle discipline is the lever.

  5. P&L path

    Revenue scale drowns the early investment. Gross revenue rises from ~US$36k (2026) to ~US$8.8M (2028); EBIT impact is negative in 2026, crosses to ~US$152k in 2027, and reaches ~US$1.35M in 2028.

  6. Commercial org

    Launch with a Head to build a foundation, plus 3 AEs in 2026 (investment of ~US$86k). Add Argentina leadership and scale AEs and AMs with volume—~20 FTE by EoY 2028.
